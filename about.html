How we operate

協議会の運営原則と範囲。

Principles
01ON THE LINKS serves as Council representative for external relations and operations.
02Technical drafts and public materials are reviewed by participating members before release.
03Reference technologies — including Responsibility OS and ADIC — are open, not mandatory. The Council is not a sales or certification channel for any single organization.
04Validated findings and future proposals are clearly distinguished in all publications.
Formal governance — decision procedures, voting rights, IP policy — is being developed with founding members. Contact us with questions.意思決定手続き・IP方針は整備中です。
What we do not do
—Represent Hiroshima Prefecture, Hiroshima City, or any government body.
—Represent or act on behalf of the Hiroshima AI Process.
—Provide legal certification, compliance guarantees, or safety endorsements.
—Require adoption of any specific technology as a condition of participation.
—Promise that all AI outcomes can be controlled or accountability automatically satisfied.
03
Why Hiroshima

なぜ広島から始めるのか。

Hiroshima is known internationally as the city where the Hiroshima AI Process originated (G7, 2023). The Council is independently operated and does not represent that governmental process — or any government authority.

Its focus is narrower: translating principles of trustworthy AI into real operational conditions, beginning with Hiroshima-based logistics. Starting here reflects a concrete choice — ON THE LINKS, the Council's representative, is rooted in the city, and implementation begins where real expertise and real operations exist.

広島は、2023年のG7サミットを契機に広島AIプロセスが生まれた都市として国際的に知られています。本協議会はこの政府プロセスや行政機関を代表するものではなく、民間主導で独立して運営されています。

協議会の目的はより具体的です。信頼できるAIに関する理念を、広島の物流現場から具体的な運用条件へ落とし込むことです。代表企業・オンザリンクスが広島に根ざしているからこそ、実装はここから始まります。
